Wiley Hub Guide

The Hub Guide is a documentation site that helps both Wiley employees and society executives understand the abilities of the Hub platform.

It includes information on the hub website structure, as well as a list of site elements such as widgets, carousels, branding, etc.

A special feature of the site is the ability to change the colours of the wire frames to suit a prospective customer’s brand.
