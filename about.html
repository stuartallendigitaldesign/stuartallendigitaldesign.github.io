About

Currently, I am employed as a Full-time UI developer and AV specialist; and freelance web and graphic designer based in Oxfordshire, UK.

I have 7 years professional experience in website design (wireframing, HTML5, CSS3 (w/ SASS), Javascript), illustration, moving-image, and graphic design.

The wide spectrum of skills I have acquired allow me to see a project through from start to finish; from the realisation of a new brand, to the construction of a fully-featured website.

My aim is to produce a coherent, effective interface between your company and their audience.

For more information contact me on sallen@stuartallen.design
